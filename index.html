John Fraser Arts Council

Who we are Our events

A team of students...

with the goal of fostering an inclusive artistic community at Fraser, providing a platform for emerging talents to shine. The council connects artists and enthusiasts alike.

Events we host:


Coffeehouses

Whether you're a musician, poet, comedian, or dancer, this setting provides the perfect stage to showcase your skills and enjoy a drink with fellow art enthusiasts.

Arts night

An evening dedicated to the arts. This event showcases a diverse range of artistic expressions from all arts departments, including drama, dance, music, visual arts, media arts, etc.

Arts banquet

A fun night of celebration, recognition, and awards to honor the achievements and contributions of talented artists. Featuring YOUR art displays and an artistic atmosphere that radiates creativity.

Workshops

Hands-on workshop sessions for all kinds of art, led by Council members who provide valuable insights, techniques, and inspiration to help you develop your craft and find your next hobby!

Karaoke

Let your voice shine at our popular and exciting karaoke event. Grab the mic, choose your favorite songs, and sing your heart out in a lively atmosphere!

Career talks

Gain invaluable insights and guidance from professionals in the arts. Discover the diverse career paths within the arts and turn your passion into a fulfilling career.


And more!
