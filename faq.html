Why should I apply?

Arts Council provides a rewarding opportunity for students to actively contribute to the arts in our community and make a meaningful impact. By becoming a council member, you'll bring your unique skills, ideas, and perspectives to the table, helping us make important decisions and run exciting initiatives. You'll have the chance to grow personally, develop valuable connections with fellow members, and the opportunity to see your artistic ideas brought to life.

How do I apply to join the council?

We take pride in the transparency of our application process! To join the council, please fill out the first round written application document that you can access through our Instagram or on the top right corner of our website. Please follow the instructions on the docs and email it to jfssarts@gmail.com once completed. After the due date, the executives will examine the applications through a holistic review process, and successful applicants will be invited to round 2 interviews with the team. Feel free to message the Instagram account @jfssarts for application questions and/or feedback. Good luck!

What are the responsibilities and expectations of council members?

Council members are expected to attend weekly meetings on Thursdays at lunch and actively participate in decision-making processes. They're also expected to attend events and collaborate effectively with fellow council members, staff, and the school community. This includes working together to achieve goals and plan events. Members will also be expected to commit the necessary time and effort to fulfill their individual role responsibilities.

Are there any specific qualifications or requirements to join?

No! Anyone from any grade can apply as long as you have some sort of connection to one or more of the arts. This most commonly includes self-learning, taking classes outside of school, or taking an arts class in Fraser.
